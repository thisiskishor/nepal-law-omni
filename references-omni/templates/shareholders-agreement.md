### 3.1 Right of First Refusal (ROFR)
Before transferring any shares to a third party, a Shareholder ("Offeror") must first offer those shares to the remaining Shareholders ("Offerees") at the same price and terms as the proposed third-party transfer.

- Offer Notice: in writing, specifying price, number of shares, proposed buyer
- Response Period: **[30] days** from receipt of Offer Notice
- If Offerees decline or do not respond within 30 days, the Offeror may transfer to the third party at no lower price
