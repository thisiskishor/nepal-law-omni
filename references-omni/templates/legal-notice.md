## WHY A LEGAL NOTICE MATTERS

In Nepal, courts expect parties to have made reasonable attempts to resolve disputes before litigation. A properly served Legal Notice:
- Establishes the claimant's position on record
- Starts the clock on any response period
- Demonstrates good faith / pre-litigation effort
- Creates admissible evidence of the demand and non-response
- Is mandatory in certain statutory claims (verify per specific Act)
