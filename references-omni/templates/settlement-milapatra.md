## WHY MILAPATRA MATTERS

The Milapatra (Compromise Deed) is one of the most frequently used legal instruments in Nepal. It:
- Settles disputes out of court (or during court proceedings)
- Once registered or filed before a court, is enforceable as a decree
- Saves time and cost of full litigation
- Is recognized under the Muluki Civil Procedure Code 2074 as a valid mode of dispute resolution
- Can be used for property disputes, money claims, family matters, business disagreements
