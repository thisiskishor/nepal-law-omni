# Governing Law: Labour Act 2074 (2017 AD), Secs. 42–50; Labour Regulation 2075

## TERMINATION TYPES UNDER LABOUR ACT 2074

| Type | Grounds | Notice Required | Compensation |
|------|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Termination with notice** | Redundancy, restructuring, non-performance | As per contract or 30 days minimum | Gratuity + unused leave encashment |
| **Termination for cause** | Gross misconduct (Sec. 42) | None — immediate | Gratuity only (no notice pay) |
| **Retrenchment** | Business closure, economic downsurn | 30 days | Gratuity + retrenchment compensation |
| **Expiry of fixed term** | End of contract term | As per contract | Gratuity if 1+ year served |
| **Retirement** | Age / mutual agreement | As per policy | Gratuity + all dues |

**Gross Misconduct Grounds (Sec. 42 — immediate termination):**
- Theft, fraud, or financial dishonesty
- Physical assault on employer, colleague, or customer
- Deliberate damage to company property
- Conviction for criminal offense involving moral turpitude
- Serious violation of workplace safety rules causing risk to life
- Disclosure of confidential business information
- Habitual absence without approval (verify current definition)

**For all other terminations:** Must follow the warning → show cause → inquiry process first.
